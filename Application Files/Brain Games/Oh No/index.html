About

0h n0 is a little logic game
that complements 0h h1.
It was created by
Magnus Marx.

The concept is inspired by
the Japanese Kuromasu.

Rules

Blue dots can see others
in their own row and column.
They see at least one.

Their numbers tell how many.

Red dots block their view.

This is a message.
